Running Tips

Strength

Strength training is an important part of injury prevention. Focus on your core— especially your abs and gluts

Cadence

Check your stride turnover. The most efficient runners take about 180 steps per minute.

Change it up

Don't run the same everytime you hit the road. Vary your pace. Vary the distance of your runs.

Focus on form

Run tall but relax. Your feet should hit the ground beneath your hips. Not out in front of you.
